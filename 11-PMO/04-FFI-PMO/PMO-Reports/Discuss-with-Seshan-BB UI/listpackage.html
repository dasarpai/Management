CHOOSE A PACKAGE

To begin your interview, click on the button to the right of your benefit Package. There are three packages to choose from based on your medical election.

Please note: once your are enrolled in the medical plan, you may not drop or change your plan for any reason until the next open enrollment

  • If you are electing UHC for medical, please select the “UHC” package. Changes to move to the next.
  • UHC ENROLLMENT: If you are enrolling in UHC you will automatically be enrolled in Behaviour Health through Managed Health Network and Express Scripts for pharmacy coverage.
  • If you are electing Kaiser for medical, please select the “KP” package.
  • If you are Waiving medical, please select WV package. NOTE: Classifiec must be contracted to work less than 6.25 hours per day to waive; Certificated must have less than an 80% contract to waive.
